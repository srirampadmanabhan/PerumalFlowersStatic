Our Story

A Legacy of Divine Service

Perumal Flowers is a traditional Indian Tamil flower shop that has been serving the faithful community of Abu Dhabi for over three decades.

Founded in 1990 with a small shop and a big dream, we started our journey to bring the essence of Tamil Nadu's rich floral traditions to the UAE. What began as a humble venture has now blossomed into a trusted name with 5 branches across Abu Dhabi.

Our founder's vision was simple yet profound: to provide fresh, quality flowers and authentic pooja items that help devotees maintain their spiritual practices away from their homeland. Today, we continue to honor that vision with unwavering commitment.

Our Offerings

What Makes Us Special

Fresh Flowers for God

We specialize in traditional flowers that are essential for Hindu worship and daily pooja rituals:

  • Jasmine (Malli Poo) - The queen of flowers, perfect for hair decoration and temple offerings
  • Marigold (Samanthi) - Auspicious orange blooms for garlands and decorations
  • Rose - Symbol of love and devotion for deity worship
  • Flower Garlands (Mala) - Hand-crafted garlands for god statues and special occasions
  • Loose Flowers - For daily pooja and temple visits

Complete Pooja Essentials

Everything you need for your daily worship and special ceremonies:

  • Agarbatti & Dhoop - Premium quality incense sticks and cones
  • Camphor (Karpooram) - Pure camphor for aarti
  • Kumkum & Turmeric - Essential for tilak and pooja
  • Sacred Thread & Kalava - For religious rituals
  • Brass Pooja Items - Lamps, bells, and vessels
  • Coconuts & Betel Leaves - For special ceremonies

Authentic Indian Groceries

A taste of home with our carefully curated selection:

  • Rice Varieties - Ponni, Basmati, and traditional Tamil Nadu rice
  • Lentils & Pulses - Toor dal, urad dal, moong, and more
  • Spices & Masalas - Authentic South Indian spice blends
  • Cooking Essentials - Oils, ghee, and condiments
  • Snacks & Sweets - Traditional Indian treats

Our Heritage

Rooted in Tamil Tradition

Our roots trace back to the beautiful temple towns of Tamil Nadu, where flowers are not just decorations but an integral part of spiritual life. From the jasmine-adorned hair of Tamil women to the magnificent garlands adorning temple deities, flowers hold a sacred place in our culture.

At Perumal Flowers, we bring this rich tradition to Abu Dhabi. Whether it's for your daily pooja, a special festival like Pongal or Diwali, or a family celebration, we ensure you have access to the authentic flowers and items that connect you to your roots.
